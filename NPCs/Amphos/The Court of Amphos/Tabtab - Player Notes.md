**Tabtab** is a court-affiliated apothecary known to be in service to [[The Prince - Player Notes|the Prince]], operating within elite political circles. She is recognised by the party as an intelligent, perceptive, and socially dangerous figure with access to sensitive information.

## Overview

Tabtab is described as a **“catfire” (Tabaxi / Half-Elf hybrid)**, marking her as a rare and visually distinctive individual. She functions as an apothecary and is closely tied to the Prince’s court, placing her under significant political protection.

## Known Activities

- Serves as an apothecary to the Prince, indicating access to both medicinal knowledge and high-ranking individuals.
- Moves within elite or courtly environments, suggesting influence beyond her profession.
- Demonstrates high intelligence and awareness, particularly in social interactions.

## Interaction with the Party

## Session 2

Tabtab is found unconscious in her room surrounded by miscellaneous plants and herbs, an apothecary's kit and numerous empty bottles of alcohol. One of the party found her diary on the floor that details medication and dosages for the prince to aid in his headache recovery.

## Session 4

Tabtab’s primary interaction with the party occurs during a social encounter where she:
- Immediately recognises [[Ellowen]], addressing her as _“Lady Elowen.”_
- References locations including [[Brackenfall]] and [[Aurelian Keep]].
- Engages confidently in conversation, nearly exposing sensitive information.
During the same encounter:
- An attempt by [[Riley]] to manipulate or deceive her fails.
- An attempted theft of her belongings is unsuccessful.

## Abilities and Traits

- Highly perceptive
	- Able to identify individuals and their backgrounds with minimal information.
- Intellectually capable
	- Resistant to manipulation and deception.
- Socially adept
	- Operates confidently in high-risk conversational situations.
- Politically protected
	- Association with the Prince makes direct action against her difficult.
- Educated
	- Her knowledge of medicine, political entities, and ongoing political situations means she is well-educated.
