**The Prince** is an heir to the throne of the [[Solaran Empire]] (often referred to as the _Eastern Solaran Empire_ or _Amphosian Despotate_). Ruling from the capital city of [[Amphos]], he is a central figure in the regime that maintains the laws and prestige of the old Solaran Empire.

# Background

# Association with the Party

## Session 1
The party are currently fugitives on the run after successfully completing a heist at a temple in Amphos in order to obtain their [[Elements#Elemental Stones|Elemental Stones]] and [[Ellowen's Book]]. Following this Ellowen retreated to the [[Academy of the Arcane in Amphos]] where [[Riley]] triggered a protection mechanism on the [[Elements#Elemental Stones#Fire Stone|Fire Stone]] resulting in [[Big Beefy Bob]] obtaining the [[Elements#Elemental Stones#Soul Stone|Soul Stone]], alerting the [[Head of the Arcane]] and leading to an official arrest warrant issued by the Prince's government.

Once rested in [[The Heavy Head Inn]], [[Ellowen]] later pulls out [[Ellowen's Betrothal Letter - Player Notes|her betrothal letter]] which states that she is to be betrothed to the prince of Amphos.

## Session 2
When the party woke up the following day in the inn just north of Amphos, there was a mysterious hooded figure seated alone in the upper landing writing. Ellowen later discerned that this was indeed the prince but had neglected to remember that this was her betrothed. Due to Riley's hatred of all thing royal she refused to inform the rest of the party to this matter. Big Beefy Bob was none the wiser to his disguise and nor was Riley. Bob thought he was hungry and started feeding him gruel. From this interaction Big Beefy Bob has declared him his "second bestest bestie" alongside Ellowen as his "first bestest bestie."

## Session 4
The party encountered him and [[Tabtab - Player Notes|his apothecary companion]] within [[The Panting Shadow]], where they were involved in the game of cups, the prince was quiet, despite Big Beefy Bob's approaches toward him. He ended up being one of the winners of the game alongside [[Ellowen]] who was growing increasingly frustrated at Tabtab. When they left he beckoned Tabtab to follow him.

## Session 5
The party could briefly see a royal ship headed towards [[Itrea]] while they were on ship.
