**Wrensu** is a mysterious figure, associate and adoptive father of [[Riley]], acting as a handler and coordinator for various illicit activities within the city of [[Amphos]].

## Overview
Wrensu operates from a shared residence or [[Riley's House|"house"]] with Riley, where he monitors the movements of his associates and maintains a network of informants. He appears well-connected, as his contacts are able to track Riley’s location even when he is taken into custody by the City Watch.

## Known Activities
Wrensu is primarily responsible for identifying high-value targets and artifacts for Riley to acquire. Known missions coordinated by Wrensu include:
* **The Great Palace Heist:** Wrensu tasked Riley with infiltrating the archives of [[The Great Palace of Amphos]] to steal an artifact holding sensitive secrets. He specifically chose Riley for this mission because of the boy's speed, small stature, and ability to "pass as a girl" to avoid detection.
* **The Temple to Tolaume:** Wrensu informed Riley of [[Elements#Elemental Stones|Elemental Stones]] being transported to the temple and advised that they would need "muscle" (extra strength) to successfully break into the location.

## Relationships
* **Riley:** Wrensu is Riley's adoptive father and acts as Riley's primary contact and mission provider. He relies on Riley’s unique skills as a courier and thief, often dealing with the logistical and information-gathering side of their operations.
* **Merchant Contacts:** Wrensu coordinates with various merchant contacts who pay "in kind" for the delivery of stolen documents and messages.

## Personality and Traits
Wrensu is depicted as a pragmatic and cautious individual. He is quick to notice when a plan has gone awry, such as when Riley failed to deliver papers after being intercepted by a City Watch officer who was monitoring the recipient. He possesses a keen understanding of the local military and political movements, allowing him to stay ahead of government activities.
