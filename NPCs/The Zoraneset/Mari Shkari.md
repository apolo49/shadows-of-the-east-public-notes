**Mari Shkari** was the captain of the imperial warship _[[The Zoraneset]]_, overseeing its voyage during the party’s journey across open waters. He was a figure of authority aboard the ship, responsible for command, discipline, and the safety of both crew and passengers.

His tenure as captain came to a sudden and violent end when he was found murdered under mysterious circumstances during the voyage.

## **Role in the Voyage**

Mari Shkari commanded _The Zoraneset_, a large imperial warship operating below full crew capacity. Due to this shortage, he assigned members of the party to officer roles to help maintain order and functionality aboard the vessel.

Under his leadership:

- The ship continued its journey despite dangerous conditions, including supernatural events and sea monsters.
- The party was integrated into the ship’s command structure.
- Discipline and structure were maintained among a strained and understaffed crew.

## **Personality & Leadership**

From player interactions and observations, Mari Shkari appeared to be:

- **Authoritative** – Maintained strict control over the crew and expected discipline.
- **Pragmatic** – Willing to rely on outsiders (the party) when necessary.
- **Knowledgeable** – Shared historical and mythological insights, particularly regarding powerful elemental figures and past events.
- **Direct** – Spoke plainly and did not shy away from difficult or uncomfortable topics.

He was not universally liked, and tensions existed between him and several crew members.

## **Known Interactions**

During the voyage, Mari Shkari:

- Spoke with crew and party members about rumours and political events, including suspicious activities in [[Campaign 1 - Before the Start/DM Notes/Settlements/Cities/Amphos]].
- Shared stories and warnings about powerful forces tied to elemental magic and history.
- Enforced ship discipline, occasionally clashing with crew and party members.

Some individuals aboard the ship had personal grievances against him, ranging from professional disagreements to more personal conflicts.

## **Death**

Mari Shkari was discovered dead in the early hours of the morning during the voyage.

### **Circumstances**

- His body was found near the ship’s lower deck stairs.
- The scene showed signs of **violence and struggle**, including multiple wounds and evidence that the body had been **moved or dragged**.
- There were indications of a **deliberate attempt to clean the scene**, including the use of stolen alcohol.

### **Aftermath**

- The ship descended into **panic and suspicion**, with multiple crew members implicated.
- An investigation led by the party identified a primary suspect.
- Upon reaching port, the suspect was handed over to local authorities.
- [[Ellowen]] used Disguise Self to assume the role of the dead Mari Shkari to solve the murder, upon discovery by [[Big Beefy Bob]] he claims that he has "a lot of blood" and that it "runs in the family."

Mari Shkari’s death marked a turning point in the voyage, destabilising command and forcing the party into a leadership and investigative role.

## **Legacy**

Though his time with the party was brief, Mari Shkari’s impact was significant:

- He established the **command structure** that allowed the ship to function.
- His death triggered a **major investigation**, testing the party’s judgment and cohesion.
- His stories and warnings hinted at **larger forces at play** in the world.

His murder remains one of the first major crises faced by the party at sea.
