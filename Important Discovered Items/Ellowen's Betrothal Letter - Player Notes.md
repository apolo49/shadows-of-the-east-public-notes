To [[Alaric Aurelian Vale]], Duke of [[Brackenfall]], and to the House that bears his name,

The Sun moves, and the world follows.  
Know that the [[Solara|Empire of the Solari]] is attentive to its borders, and mindful of those houses whose blood has long stood between order and shadow. The Vale has endured because it has understood this truth.  
By the same understanding, I now set forth what has already been measured, weighed, and found consonant with the will of Heaven.  
Your daughter, [[Ellowen|Lady Ellowen Aurelian Vale]], is named to stand beside my son, [[The Prince - Player Notes|Prince Tukue of House Potes]], Koronuganamon, and marked from birth for the continuance of the ancient imperial line of great Solari emperors.  
Lady Ellowen is therefore acknowledged as betrothed to the Prince of the Solari and shall be received in due season as Princess-Consort of the Solari, her person and issue thereafter bound to the great, ancient, Solari imperial law, protection, and destiny.  
The House of Aurelian Vale is, by this union, drawn from the Empire’s edge into its blood. Its lands shall stand under the Sun’s regard. Its name shall be spoken among kin. Its future shall be ordered and it shall evermore be protected by the [[Imperial Army of the Eastern Solaran Empire|Tagaminase]].  
The rites of union shall be observed during High Summer, at the Festival of the [[Unconquered Sun]], within the [[Solar Basilica of Amphos]], temple to the pantheon, where emperors are crowned and time is kept. Preparations are already underway, as is fitting.  
[[Solaris Vanguard|The Vanguard]] will arrive when the season turns and will escort her thusly. They will not announce themselves twice.  
You will return your sealed affirmation before the sun completes its next great turning, that all things may proceed without disturbance and the Empire may remain whole in its course.  
As has been decreed by the great god [[Solaum (god)|Solaum]] of [[Campaign 2 - Shadows of the East/Player Notes/Settlements/Solaum|the great city]] and the Solari Empire who has given us his blessing for our future, and let the great god [[Nari]], god of war, be absent from our futures.

From [[Bardashes|Bardashes]], Koronuganamon, Mag Hirag ta Puli Solari.
