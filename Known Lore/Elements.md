# Elemental Stones

Across Tara, there are said to be ancient stones that bind themselves to their chosen possessors. It is unknown why they choose the people they do or what they intend. Each carries a fragment of something older than kingdoms, older than memory. To hold one is not to wield power, but to be shaped by it.

## Fire Stone
_main article: [[Fire]]_

The Fire Stone burns with urgency. It thrives on action, impulse, and the refusal to remain still. Those it binds to often feel a constant pressure to move, to act, to _do something_, even when stillness would be wiser.

Its power is destructive, but also liberating. It can tear through obstacles as easily as it can burn bridges. Those who carry it often find themselves walking a fine line between freedom and self-destruction.

## Water Stone
_main article: [[Water]]_

The Water Stone does not act - it _responds_ in times it wants to. Those bound to it often find their magic slipping free as though magic flows out them like water from a spilt cup. It is tied to instinct, emotion, and the pull of unseen currents.

Bearers speak of dreams, voices, and a growing sense that they are no longer entirely separate from the tides they command. The Water Stone rewards surrender as much as control, and it is not always clear which one it prefers.

## Earth Stone
_main article: [[Earth]]_

The Earth Stone is patient, but never passive. It is tied to the weight of the world: the slow, inevitable force of growth, decay, and change. Those bound to it may feel a deepening connection to the land itself, as though the ground listens as much as it holds.

## Soul Stone
_main article: [[Soul]]_

The Soul Stone is unlike the others. It reveals, remembers, and _reaches_. Those bound to it often experience visions, echoes, or presences that feel neither fully alive nor entirely gone.

It is tied to death, memory, and whatever lingers between. Bearers may find themselves seeing what others cannot, or being seen by things they do not understand. Whether this is a gift, a burden, or a warning is left for each to decide.

## Power Stone
_main article: [[Power]]_

This force is less understood, but no less real. It manifests not as an element, but as raw, embodied strength, discipline made manifest, will given form. It does not whisper or guide. It demands. And in return, it offers the simplest promise of all: that strength, if pursued without compromise, can reshape the world.
