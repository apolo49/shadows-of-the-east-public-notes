**Rhoda of the Shimmering Moontide**, also known as “The Luminous Bard,” is a merfairy performer and wandering storyteller who originates from the bioluminescent reefs of the [[Shimmering Moonrise Reefs]]. Raised among ancient songs, spirits, and oceanic traditions, she leaves her homeland to pursue a life of freedom and expression beyond the constraints of the [[Council of Tides]].

Defined by her vibrant appearance and deep connection to music and lore, Rhoda carries forward forgotten histories and magical traditions through performance. Though she currently lacks an [[Elements#Elemental Stones|elemental stone]] and elemental powers, unlike her companions, her knowledge, artistry, and outsider perspective position her as a unique and culturally significant member of the party.

# Backstory
Rhoda was born beneath a full moon above the glowing waters of the [[Shimmering Moonrise Reefs]], where coral towers shimmered like fallen stars and bioluminescent gardens lit the deep like constellations.

From the moment she could swim, Rhoda was different.

Her hair grew wild and fiery. A mane of red and orange like living flame against the blue glow of the reef. She inked her skin with swirling lunar symbols and ocean creatures, piercing fins and ears with bits of polished shell and silver salvaged from shipwrecks. While other merfolk dressed in pearl silks and ceremonial robes, Rhoda wore drift-leather, beads, feathers, and charms gathered from [[The Coral Bazaar]].

Her first drum was built in [[The Glimmering Caverns]], where glowing shells sang when struck together. She bound them around a hollow driftwood core, creating a radiant instrument that pulsed with light like a heartbeat. When she played, small motes of magic, starry wisps and dancing lights, would rise and spin around her, responding to rhythm and emotion.

At [[The Starlight Gardens]], she listened to the whispered histories of wandering spirits. In [[The Tranquil Grottos]], she comforted grieving families with gentle melodies on her flute. At [[Pearlforge]], she learned ancient lore songs once used to record wars, treaties, and forgotten heroes. Her mind soaked up legend, truth, and hidden knowledge.

But Rhoda’s spirit was too wild for the rigid [[Council of Tides]].

Rather than submit, she rose toward the surface moonlight and began life as a wandering performer. On land, she became known as "The Luminous Bard".
