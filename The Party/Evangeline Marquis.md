# Backstory

# In the Campaign

## Session 1 — The Stones in Amphos

Yvaquis enters the story in the [[Northern Slums]] of [[Amphos]], travelling with a small band of companions who catch wind of a rumour about the [[Elements#Elemental Stones|Elemental Stones]] being stored at the [[Temple to Tolaume]], specifically, that one of the stones has the peculiar property of perfectly grinding herbs. Intrigued, and by fortune or fate, she finds herself aligned with a group of strangers converging on the same temple that night.

At midnight, while [[Riley - Player Notes|Riley]] gains entry through the roof and [[Ellowen Aurelian Vale|Ellowen]] approaches the vault door, Yvaquis simply walks in through the front entrance, after it was cleared by [[Big Beefy Bob - Player Notes|Big Beefy Bob]]. Inside the inner chamber, she follows Ellowen deeper into the vault, where she is inexplicably drawn to the [[Elements#Earth Stone|Earth Stone]]. The stone chooses her as much as she chooses it. Almost immediately, it begins to speak to her directly through her mind — an experience so disorienting that Yvaquis initially suspects she has lost her sanity.

In the chaos following the heist, the party make their way to the [[Academy of the Arcane in Amphos]] alongside Ellowen and Big Beefy Bob, while the [[Head of the Arcane]] orders all possessors of the stones arrested. The group flee north-west through the slums and escape Amphos together, becoming wanted fugitives. At [[The Heavy Head Inn]] along the road to [[Aklebe]], the party's uneasy mutual secrecy is on full display — Yvaquis asks Ellowen about her unusually clean and wealthy appearance, and Ellowen asks the same of her. Neither reveals anything.

## Session 3 — The Path

Travelling north along the road to Aklebe, the party's journey through quiet countryside turns meaningful and then violent. They discover a broken tombstone bearing the name of the legendary ancient traveller [[Hasharshagetes]], and an ancient stone circle left by the Bon people — its stones covered in mosses of elemental colours, the area charged with latent energy.

Their passage through dense forest brings an ambush by a pack of wolves. In the ensuing combat, Yvaquis channels her clerical power decisively, destroying one wolf outright with divine energy. The party survive, though Big Beefy Bob is left critically injured and several members require both mundane and magical healing before they can continue.

Later, the group discover an enormous and impossibly ancient golden tree growing in a forest clearing, its roots drawing on magically charged stones deep underground. When Yvaquis touches the tree, she experiences an overwhelming moment of elemental communion — sensing the planet as an interconnected whole, with every piece of land, stone, and soil connected through forces she is only beginning to understand. The Earth Stone's power briefly manifests physically: the terrain beneath her feet deforms, the ground reshaping itself around her. The experience is as unsettling as it is revelatory. The party arrive in Aklebe shaken, altered, and carrying the weight of things they cannot yet fully name.

## Session 4 — Aklebe, The Panting Shadow, and a Game of Cups

In the gambling den known as [[The Panting Shadow]], the party set about winning back the [[The Zoraneset|Harbormaster's ship]]. Yvaquis participates in the den's high-stakes Poison Roulette game — 28 drinks, six of them laced with mugwort — and, alongside Big Beefy Bob, absorbs the worst of the poisoning, eventually falling into a groggy and poisoned stupor. Despite the ordeal, the party successfully secure the Imperial War Galley _The Zoraneset_ for 50 gold and depart Aklebe with a new ally, the Firbolg [[Masha|Masha]], in tow.

## Session 5 — The Zoraneset

Aboard the newly acquired _Zoraneset_, the party assume officer roles to manage the understaffed 120-foot warship. Yvaquis falls naturally into the role of ship's Surgeon, beginning to tend to the crew.

Her secret almost does not survive the voyage intact. When Masha, a fellow traveller from [[Kingdom of Eivia|Eivia]], mentions a missing princess named Evangeline, Yvaquis involuntarily reacts to the name — an instinctive reaction and heavily hints to the group that she is, in fact, the runaway Princess Evangeline Marquis of Eivia, if they had listened. Riley had already found [[Missing Person's Poster for Evangeline Marquis|a missing person's poster bearing her name and face in Amphos]].

When a Giant Octopus attacks the ship, smashing oars and breaching the hull, Yvaquis responds in combat — damaging the creature with her _Divine Spark_ channel divinity. The beast is eventually killed, and the party eat it that evening.

## Session 6 — Murder on The Zoraneset

The session opens at 5:00 AM with a scream. Captain [[Mari Shkari]] is found murdered on the lower deck, his body apparently cleaned with stolen spirits in an attempt to conceal the crime. Yvaquis takes on a critical investigative role, performing the formal medical examinations that confirm the Captain's death and establish the physical evidence. As ship's surgeon she is one of the few figures with both the skill and the access to do so.

She is not without her own place in the suspect table — the Captain had previously confiscated her herbs and ordered her to stop smoking. She was also assigned to the 01:00 watch alongside Masha. The investigation ultimately points to the crew member [[Dashos]], who was in possession of Yvaquis's herbs. The party hand Dashos over to local authorities when the _Zoraneset_ docks at the port city of [[Pithertara]].
