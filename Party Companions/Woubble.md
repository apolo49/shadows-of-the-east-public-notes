Traits
 **_Adorably Acidic._** A creature that touches Woubble or hits him with an attack while within 5 feet of him takes 1 acid damage.
 **_Gelatinous._** A creature within 5 feet of Woubble can take an action to pull an creature or object out of Woubble. Doing so requires a successful DC 8 Strength check, and the creature making the attempt takes 2 (1d4) acid damage.
 Woubble can hold up to one Tiny creature or four Tiny objects at a time. A creature or object inside Woubble can be seen but has total cover.
 **_Bouncy._** Woubble's long jump is up to 10 feet and his high jump is up to 10 feet, with or without a running start.
Actions
 **_Blorp Blorp._** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +0 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 1 acid damage.
 **_Huggable Barrier._** Woubble activates a protective barrier around themself, which lasts for 1 minute or until Woubble dismisses it (no action required). While the barrier is active, Woubble’s AC is 10, other creatures automatically succeed on Dexterity saving throws against Engulf, and friendly creatures that touch Woubble take no damage from their Adorably Acidic trait.
 **_Engulf._** Woubble moves up to his speed. While doing so, he can enter the space of other creatures. Whenever he enters the space of a Tiny creature, the creature must make a DC 8 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 2 (1d4) acid damage and is engulfed. The engulfed creature can’t breathe, is restrained, and takes 4 (2d4) acid damage at the start of each of Woubble’s turns. When Woubble moves, the engulfed creature moves with him. 
 An engulfed creature can try to escape by taking an action to make a DC 8 Strength check. On a success, the creature escapes and enters a space of its choice within 5 feet of Woubble.
Bonus Actions
 **_Encourage._** Woubble can take the Help action as a bonus action to assist a creature within 30 feet of him.
