A young impoverished boy of [[Campaign 1 - Before the Start/DM Notes/Cultures/Solari|Solari]] descent from the town of [[Jentan|Jantani]] in the [[Caliphate of Lqlima]]. He wondered for 270 days from Jantani to [[Nymphi]], east across the desert, entering the [[Eastern Solaran Empire]] to [[Aktos|Aktosi]], travelling further east, crossing the wild plains of the [[Raurorumia Province]] to [[Athia|Athiai]], North East to join the Everlasting Trail to [[Leipathi]] and [[Pithumemi]] ([[Iponiaros Province]]), crossing into the [[Delphidria Province]], north west to [[Agrara]], then north to [[Nauros]] and [[Paphi]] in the same province. He then continued North to cross into the province of [[Rhithon Province|Rhithon]] arriving solely at the town of [[Akhalarynt]] before continuing to [[Thergan Province]] where he went to [[Nelionti]], when he finally ended up in the province of [[Athmolis Province|Athmolis]], first arriving at [[Hubros]] then continued to [[Byrian]], [[Byblostos]], [[Ampianagri]], [[Doryrena]], [[Pyrma]], [[Byblarascu]] then finally [[Akrossa]], where he spent 3 months trying to accrue food and money to continue travelling to [[Wiesen]] in [[Brackenfall]].
